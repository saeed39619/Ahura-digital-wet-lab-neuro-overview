# Ahura: Digital Wet Lab Neuro

Public overview of a confidential AI platform designed to support neurotherapeutic discovery, translational modeling, and scientific decision workflows.

## Overview
Ahura Digital Wet Lab Neuro is a scientific AI platform concept designed to support neuro-focused therapeutic research through computational workflow orchestration, structured evidence integration, and cross-functional decision support. The platform is intended to help research teams move more efficiently from complex biological questions to prioritized computational insight.

## Selected Application Areas
- **Neurology** — support for neurodegenerative and neurotherapeutic research workflows
- **Translational Science** — structured evaluation of candidate paths across preclinical research programs
- **Precision Medicine** — computational support for target- and context-specific research strategies
- **Platform Adaptation** — extensible scientific framework for additional therapeutic areas where appropriate

## Platform Focus
- AI-assisted scientific workflow support
- Computational prioritization and translational decision support
- Structured evidence integration across research stages
- Scalable analysis and reporting for life sciences R&D
- Cross-functional visibility for scientific and technical teams

## Why It Matters
Neurotherapeutic research often requires integration across multiple layers of scientific evidence, from mechanistic reasoning to translational interpretation. Ahura Digital Wet Lab Neuro reduces friction in that process by improving how research workflows are structured, prioritized, and turned into actionable computational insights.

## Representative Use Cases
- Neurotherapeutic research support and candidate prioritization
- Computational decision support for translational programs
- Structured analysis and reporting for internal scientific review
- AI-guided workflow support for multidisciplinary R&D teams
